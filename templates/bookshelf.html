Skunk Works: A Personal Memoir of My Years at Lockheed

Ben R. Rich & Leo Janos · December 2024

Offers an insider's view of Lockheed's legendary aerospace division. As Kelly Johnson's successor, Rich shares both the technical triumphs and human stories, from the engineers who designed them to the pilots who tested them. If you're looking to be inspired by ambitious technical projects, check out Skunk Works. Every time I revisit this book, I'm blown away by what was achieved. The story is beautifully written, with interviews that provide firsthand accounts of the missions these planes undertook.
